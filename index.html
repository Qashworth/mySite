About

I'm a Software Engineer based in Denver, CO

My passions are problem solving and creating new things, whether that's in the form of elegant and innovative code or treacherous D&D encounters. My desire to build has me constantly thinking about code, which means inspiration strikes at interesting times. Whether it's at a baseball game, painting miniatures, setting up a board game or pulling a homemade pizza out of the oven, I'm often itching to get back in front of the computer to put my new idea to the test.

From Baltimore, MD (Go O's!) and always looking for the next adventure.
